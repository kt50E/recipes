Welcome to TLDR Kitchen!

I've always loved cooking and following great recipes online, but the endless scrolling and "Jump to Recipe" buttons sometimes take the fun out of it. So my husband and I decided to create this little website together through what I like to call vibe-coding.

We're not professional coders, so the site isn't perfect, but it's perfect for my recipe diary. I hope you find it useful and enjoy cooking along.
